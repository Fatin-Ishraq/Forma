ABOUT FORMA

Cellular automata sandbox

What this is

Forma is a browser sandbox for exploring emergent behavior on a high-resolution grid. Paint cells, change rules, export stills, and switch visual themes to turn the same simulation into very different atmospheres.

Modes

Conway: classic life-like automata using birth and survival masks such as B3/S23.

Generations: living cells age through multiple states, leaving structured trails and pulses.

Ambient: a calmer viewing mode with a curated seed, slow speed, hidden UI, and gentle camera drift.

Controls

Left drag: paint

Right drag: erase

Middle drag: pan

Wheel: zoom

Touch: one finger paints, two fingers pan and pinch to zoom

Zoom buttons: step in, out, or reset

Shortcuts

A: toggle ambient mode

D: cycle theme

F: toggle presentation mode

Space: play/pause simulation

R: cycle resolution mode (auto, 512, 1024)

+ / -: zoom in or out

0: reset zoom

?: open About

Esc: close About
